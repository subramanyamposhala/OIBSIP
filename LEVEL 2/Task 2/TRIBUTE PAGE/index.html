A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."

Biography

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was intimately involved in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts, earning him the nickname "Missile Man of India". He played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998. Known for his humility, he inspired millions of young Indians with his vision for India and his motivational speeches.
